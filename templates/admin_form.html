How Filtering Works

Candidates must meet at least 3 out of 4 criteria to pass:

  • Total experience requirement
  • Position-specific experience requirement
  • Position match (at least one candidate experience must match expected positions)
  • Education requirement

Qualified candidates will be sent to the qualified email address, while unqualified candidates will be sent to the unqualified email address. Both will include details and resume attached.
